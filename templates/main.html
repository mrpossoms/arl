Who are we?

We are hobbyists, students, enthusiasts and professional engineers who make up an encouraging environment for those who are passionate about autonomous robotics and racing. We offer a low-stakes, reoccuring event that encourages you to build, race and improve autonomous racing robots. Anyone of any skill level is welcome. Come learn, create and compete with us!

What are we doing?

We are building and racing robots! Currently we are specifically building around 1:10 scale and smaller electric RC models. This allows us to race year round, both inside and outdoors.
